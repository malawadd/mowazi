# Moeazi Landing Video Design

## Style Prompt
Make the motion feel like an operator’s control surface brought to life. Flat neobrutalist color, hard black structure, square geometry, and bold typography should stay intact. The videos should feel stamped, direct, and product-led rather than glossy or cinematic in a generic SaaS way.

## Colors
- `#FFFDF5` paper background
- `#FFD23F` shell yellow
- `#74B9FF` overview accent
- `#88D498` wallet and withdrawal accent
- `#FFA552` operator action accent
- `#FF7BA5` risk and activity accent
- `#B8A9FA` audit accent
- `#111111` ink and structure

## Typography
- Display: `Syne`
- UI and body: `Plus Jakarta Sans`
- Labels and meta: `IBM Plex Mono`

## Components
- Heavy 5px near-black border for main frames
- Small 3px border for inset notes and callouts
- Hard shadow blocks, never glow or blur cards
- Screenshot windows framed like product slabs, not browser chrome toys
- Small mono labels for scene tags and callouts

## Motion
- Use short hard-edged entrances, then settle into slow product motion
- Screenshots get pan or zoom, never sit completely still
- Callouts should stamp in, not float softly
- Trust-model arrows should pulse with clear directionality

## What Not To Do
- No gradients, glass, blur washes, or soft atmospheric glow
- No rounded corners
- No generic dark tech aesthetic
- No long paragraphs inside the videos
- No fake dashboard art when a real captured product surface exists
